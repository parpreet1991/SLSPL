Silver Leaf Solutions

We take this opportunity to introduce Silver Leaf Solutions as an Independent Global IT & Licensing Advisory, Services & Strategic Software Sourcing Company that is focused on enabling businesses with knowledge and methodology that helps drive effective and efficient IT decisions.

With our team having an extensive experience in working with global business advisory, networking and software publisher companies, we help customers with the in-depth knowledge and transparency on licensing solutions (Software and Hardware) and technology advisory services that can deliver cost effective solutions and forward-thinking approach.

We specialize in optimizing global contracts and provide best practices on control and governance of IT products and solutions thereby leading to a self-controlled, optimized and compliant environment at all times.
